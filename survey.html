FAQ

Is there a sugar-free version of Sprite?

Yes, Sprite Zero Sugar is a sugar-free version of the drink that offers the same refreshing taste without any sugar.

How many calories are in a can of Sprite?

A 12-ounce (355 ml) can of regular Sprite contains approximately 140 calories. However, the calorie content may vary depending on the country and formulation.

Are there any special editions or limited-time flavors of Sprite?

Yes, The Coca-Cola Company occasionally releases limited-time flavors and special editions of Sprite, which may be available in select markets.
